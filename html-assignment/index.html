About Me

I grew up in a small village called Lakefield, just outside of Peterborough. I've taken french immersion, and did a high school arts program with a focus in both visual arts and music. I have also completed an esthetics program at FC and have worked in a spa for roughly 2 years. I figure skated for most of my life until an injury prevented me from continuing, though I’ve recently been trying to learn how to roller skate. I play the ukulele, and can play some basic songs on the guitar.

I’ve always dreamed of being a content creator, whether that’s making websites and marketing campaigns, or making entertaining videos on TikTok. I’ve always been interested in design, and I love interior decorating, makeup artistry, and making collages. I’m looking forward to improving my skills in photography as it’s been 3 years since I've taken my high school photography class. So far I’ve found the programming component of this program to be very interesting and am excited to see what all I’ll be able to create with the information I learn this semester.
